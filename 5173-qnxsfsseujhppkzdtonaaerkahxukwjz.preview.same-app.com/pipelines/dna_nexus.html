DNAnexus: Cloud-Based Genomic Data Platform

DNAnexus provides a secure, scalable, and AI-powered platform for managing, analyzing, and sharing large-scale genomics and omics datasets. It accelerates research workflows and enables collaboration while maintaining compliance.

Key Features

  • Data Management: ingest, store, and integrate omics datasets securely
  • Data Analysis: deploy pipelines and AI/ML algorithms with speed and scalability
  • Collaboration: secure environment for multi-team and multi-institution research
  • Cloud-based platform for reproducible workflows
  • Supports precision health, real-world data, and biobank studies
